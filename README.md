# AFTERMAP-Dataset
AFTERMAP: A UAV-based post-disaster building damage dataset aligned with FEMA PDA damage categories.

The dataset contains post-disaster UAV images with pixel-wise annotations aligned with FEMA Preliminary Damage Assessment (PDA) damage categories.

## Dataset Access

Due to privacy, copyright, and data-use restrictions, the full dataset is not publicly redistributed through this repository. Access may be provided upon reasonable request for non-commercial academic research purposes.

## Classes

- No Damage
- Minor Damage
- Major Damage
- Destroyed
- Tarp
